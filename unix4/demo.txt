What is Grep ? 
  - searching using regex $ 
What is sed  ?
  - searching and transformation

Regex is composed of combination of wild cards like:
^ caret and $ dollar
\ escape and character class []
* asterix (zero or many)
+ plus (one or many)
? hook (zero or one)
( ) grouping and single character .

{m.n} represents ^quantity^ between m and n:
[0-9]{2,4} represents two to four digits like 22, 333, 444
